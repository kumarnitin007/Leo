Honest rating — current app look & feel

Visual polish
5.5 / 10
Cards and colors work in isolation but feel inconsistent together. The colored card backgrounds compete with each other.
Hierarchy & clarity
6 / 10
Good feature coverage and layout logic. Progress text is clear. But the action buttons (AI, Reorder, Hold) are all the same visual weight — no clear primary action.
Professional feel
5 / 10
Feels like a well-built hobbyist app rather than a product someone would pay for. The emoji-heavy cards and multicolor sections give it a "first launch" aesthetic.
