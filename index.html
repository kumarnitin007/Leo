Frequently asked questions

Is my data private and encrypted?

Yes. The Vault uses AES-256 client-side encryption, so passwords, documents, and bank records are encrypted on your device before they are ever stored or synced. Everything is unlocked with a master password that never leaves your device, and the Vault auto-locks after a session timeout.

Does Leo Planner cost anything?

Leo Planner has a generous free tier that gives you access to the full feature set. If it helps you and you'd like to support its development, donations are always appreciated via Venmo or PayPal.

What platforms does Leo Planner run on?

It runs in any modern web browser and installs as a PWA on desktop, Android, and iOS. On phones it uses a bottom navigation bar, and the app shell works offline.

Can I import my existing tasks and calendar?

Yes. You can import calendar events via .ics (for example from Google Calendar) and connect Google Tasks, Apple Reminders, and Todoist. The Vault supports import/export, and bank records support Excel import/export.

Where is my data stored, and does it work offline?

When you are signed in, data syncs to your own Supabase backend; Vault contents are encrypted before they leave your device. The PWA shell is offline-capable so the app still loads without a connection.
